# Newsletters / Blogs

Link: [https://krebsonsecurity.com/](https://krebsonsecurity.com/)

**Dark Reading** – A leading source for in-depth security news, analysis, and research. Covers vulnerabilities, threats, and best practices for security professionals.

Link: [https://www.darkreading.com/](https://www.darkreading.com/)

Link: [https://thehackernews.com/](https://thehackernews.com/)

**SANS NewsBites** – A twice-weekly newsletter summarizing the most important cybersecurity news, with expert commentary and actionable insights.

Link: [https://www.sans.org/newsletters/newsbites/](https://www.sans.org/newsletters/newsbites/)

**Infosecurity Magazine** – Offers global news, features, and analysis on the latest in cybersecurity, including interviews with industry leaders and technical deep-dives.

**Security Week’s Daily Briefing** – Provides daily briefings on significant cybersecurity news, threat intelligence, and analysis of major incidents and trends.

**CyberScoop** – Focuses on cybersecurity news at the intersection of technology, policy, and government. Great for staying up-to-date on regulatory and national security developments.

**CISO MAG** – Offers news, insights, and best practices for CISOs and security leaders. Covers governance, compliance, and risk management.

**Security Boulevard** – Aggregates top security blogs and news, providing a wide view of industry trends, research, and expert opinions.

**The CyberWire Daily Briefing** – A daily newsletter with concise news summaries, analysis, and interviews. Perfect for busy professionals needing a quick overview.

**Sophos News** – Shares practical security advice, threat intelligence, and updates on Sophos products and research.

**Lumifi Cyber Daily News (Mike Hamilton / MKH)** – Curated by Mike Hamilton, Field CISO of Lumifi, this daily newsletter delivers top cybersecurity news, breach alerts, and industry developments every weekday morning. Widely regarded as a go-to source for InfoSec professionals.

Link: [https://www.lumificyber.com/daily-news/](https://www.lumificyber.com/daily-news/)

**Security Llama** – Community-driven blog and newsletter featuring cybersecurity news, tutorials, and curated resources for learners and professionals.
